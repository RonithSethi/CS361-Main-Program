About

This website is a passion project brought to fruition through CS361 at Oregon State University meant to bring nutritional information at no cost to the everyday consumer. This info is meant to educate you on the nutritional contents of foods you may encounter on a day-to-day basis, and tell you what those nutrients within your food really mean for your health.

Nutritional info on foods comes from USDA.
